# PLTW_Capstone_SmartHinge

We are a research and development team from Lenape High School in Medford, NJ. We are affiliated
with Project Lead The Way, a four-year program designed to teach students about the engineering
design process.Our group is tasked with developing potential solutions for frequent parking lot vehicle damage, like door dings.

We ask that you please fill out this survey to the best of your ability. This will help us gain an
understanding of the market we are addressing and create the best possible solution for our project.


All information will remain anonymous, and honesty is appreciated. Thank you in advance for your
cooperation.
